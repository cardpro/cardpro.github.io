Design your card

Click "Design Giftcard" on the bar above. If on mobile, first click the icon with 3 lines. A form will show up and you must fill it.

The first feild is for the title of the card.
The second one is for the text on the first page.
The third one is for the text on the second page.
the fourth one if for the holiday or another reason you made this card,
the fifth one is for the author of the card,
and the sixth one is who the card is being given to.

Print your page

You must remember these instuctions, to open print screen press ctrl+P:

  • Set your destination to your printer
  • Set pages to all
  • Set copies to 1
  • Set layout to landscape, and set color to, "Color", and click print
  • Cut out the two card parts, cut the sections apart, and glue them so you cant see the balnk sides, then glue the two pages together to make you card
